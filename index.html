Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its rich, hearty meat sauce. It's a favorite among food lovers worldwide!

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g can of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, saut√© until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper. Toss with cooked spaghetti.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.

Tip: For a vegetarian version, replace ground beef with lentils or plant-based mince.
